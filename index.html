Consider an abortion?

Would you like to terminate your pregnancy using abortion pills?

Each year 40% of pregnancies worldwide are unplanned. This means that everyday women from all around the world face unwanted pregnancies and, for many different reasons, decide to have an abortion. All women, regardless of race, class, religion or where they live in the world deserve safe options.

We provide accurate information about safe abortion.

Do you have questions about the abortion process?

If you have questions about abortion and/or abortion pills, take a look at our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section. If you don’t find what you are looking for or you would like to talk with us directly, please contact one of our multilingual trained counselors through the Contact us page or Live Chat box. We are here to support you.
